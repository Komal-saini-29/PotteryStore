    Know Us

    Long Lasting & 100% Natural material



    Ceramic Product

    Ceramic pottery pieces are made mostly of natural clay, a few organic materials, and water.

    Stoneware Pottery

    Stoneware is made from from unrefined clay. A piece of pottery that is strong and chip resistant.

    Earthenware Pottery

    Earthenware is fired at 1915 degrees Fahrenheit, much lower than stoneware. The result is porous pottery that is not as strong as stoneware

    Porcelain Pottery

    Porcelain is made from the finest quality of white clay. It is fired at 2300 degrees Fahrenheit, resulting in hard, strong and translucent pieces of pottery.
